Step into a brighter, calmer, sunnier tomorrow

At Sunny Days, we believe that every person deserves a space where they feel seen, heard, and supported. Life can bring moments of uncertainty, stress, and emotional pain—especially during adolescence and young adulthood. But it can also bring healing, resilience, and growth. Our mission is to help you find your light, even when the skies feel cloudy.

Sunny Days offers a welcoming environment for adolescents, teens, young adults, and families to explore emotions, build coping skills, and rediscover balance. Whether you’re facing challenges with anxiety, school pressures, relationships, or life transitions, you don’t have to face them alone.

Our Specialties

  • We provide both in-person counseling in a comfortable, private setting and secure telehealth sessions for those who prefer flexibility or live outside the local area. Wherever you are, Sunny Days is here to help you take that first step toward emotional wellness and a brighter tomorrow.

Why Choose Sunny Days?

  • At Sunny Days, therapy is not a one-size-fits-all process—it’s a collaborative journey. Our approach combines empathy, evidence-based strategies, and a deep respect for each client’s individual story. We focus on the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—helping clients build resilience and self-understanding that lasts well beyond the therapy room.
  • Sunny Days was founded by Rachel Day, M.Ed., M.A., LGPC, LSC, a compassionate counselor with a passion for supporting youth and families through life’s challenges. Rachel’s warm, approachable style creates a safe space where healing and personal growth can flourish.
